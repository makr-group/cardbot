STL Files for carbot controllers

STL are designed to be printed with PLA and are untested 
with PETG, ABS, and ABS.

Printer: Ultimaker 3 Extended
Settings:
  Material: Generic PLA
  Supports: No
  Bed Adhession: 5mm Skirt
Notes:
  No supports is important to keep the USB-C port clean
  of unwanted material. Supports might be needed if your
  printer cannot handle the that lenght of bridging.
